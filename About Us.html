#Know Us

Who We Are?

VR Aircon is an Mumbai based company dealing in the field of Thermal-Cold Insulation. Mr.Anil Singh founded company in 2009 to cater to the widely untapped market of insulation. Initially it was started under the name of SINGH INSULATION which has now consolidated its Supply and application business under VR Aircon.

Along with dealing in Insulation Material we are also helping our clients in application of the said material. We have teams of various skilled people who do lagging as well as fabrication of Insulation material under our technical supervision.

We have served all kinds of insulation needs of various industries across Gujarat, & Maharashtra since last 14 years to meet the indispensable part of any industry – i.e. heat conservation.

We shape our HVAC; thereafter, they shape us
